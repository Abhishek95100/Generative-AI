Cricket is a game of cheer,
Loved by people far and near.
Bat and ball upon the ground,
Excitement and joy all around.

The bowler runs with skill and pace,
The batter stands and takes his place.
A four, a six, the crowd will roar,
Making everyone want more.

Wickets fall and catches fly,
Players always aim so high.
Through hard work and teamwork too,
Success comes to those who pursue.

Cricket teaches us to strive,
To keep our hopes and dreams alive.
Win or lose, we always learn,
For greater goals we proudly yearn.
